The Andaman Kitchen

Timing: 12:30 PM - 3:00 PM | 7:00 PM - 11:00 PM Cost: ₹3,500 per person

Experience the true essence of the islands. Our chefs work closely with local fishermen and indigenous tribes to bring forgotten recipes to life. Savor rich coconut milk curries, fresh locally foraged herbs, and traditional slow-cooked delicacies served in rustic clayware. It is a heartfelt tribute to Andaman's rich heritage.

The Grill & Tide

Timing: 6:30 PM - 11:30 PM Cost: ₹5,000 per person

Dine al fresco to the rhythmic sound of crashing waves. The Grill & Tide offers a magnificent display of the day's fresh catch alongside premium imported meats. Watch our master grillers work their magic over an open flame, infusing lobster, jumbo prawns, and Wagyu steaks with a perfect, smoky char.

SOUK

Timing: 7:00 PM - 11:45 PM Cost: ₹4,000 per person

Transport your senses to the Mediterranean and the Middle East. Under the soft glow of Moroccan lanterns, indulge in an array of hot and cold mezze, perfectly spiced shawarmas, and tender tagines. Complete your journey with traditional mint tea and delicate, honey-drenched baklava overlooking the moonlit sea.

Wasabi By Michiba

Timing: 12:30 PM - 2:45 PM | 7:00 PM - 11:30 PM Cost: ₹6,500 per person

A globally renowned culinary destination right on our private isle. Wasabi offers an exquisite exploration of authentic Japanese flavors, blending traditional techniques with modern innovation. Enjoy melt-in-your-mouth sashimi flown in fresh, artfully rolled sushi, and the legendary Black Cod Miso.

ZAIKA

Timing: 7:00 PM - 11:30 PM Cost: ₹3,800 per person

Step into an era of royal indulgence. Zaika celebrates the grandeur of Mughlai cuisine with recipes that have been passed down through generations. Rich, aromatic biryanis slow-cooked in sealed handis, melt-in-the-mouth kebabs, and velvety gravies await you in this opulent, spice-scented dining room.

Seven Rivers Brewing & Co.

Timing: 11:00 AM - 1:00 AM Cost: ₹2,500 per person

Your go-to spot for laid-back afternoons and lively evenings. We brew our own artisanal craft beers right on the island, infused with local tropical hints. Pair a chilled pint with elevated cafe classics—from wood-fired pizzas to gourmet burgers—in a relaxed, breezy environment overlooking the central pool.

Riviera Spirits Bar

Timing: 5:00 PM - 2:00 AM Cost: ₹3,000 per person

An elegant sanctuary for the connoisseur. Our mixologists craft avant-garde cocktails using premium spirits, house-made bitters, and exotic island botanicals. Sit at the onyx bar or lounge on the terrace with a rare single malt, accompanied by sophisticated tapas and the gentle sound of the ocean.

In-Villa Dining

Timing: 24 Hours Cost: A la carte + 15% service charge

For those mornings when you simply can't leave your luxurious bed, or evenings when you crave ultimate privacy. From a floating breakfast in your private infinity pool to a full multi-course dinner served on your private deck, our discreet staff brings the resort's culinary excellence directly to you.

Twilight Sands

Timing: 6:00 PM onwards (Pre-booking required) Cost: ₹15,000 per couple

The ultimate romantic gesture. We design a private, breathtaking dining setup directly on the pristine white sands. Surrounded by flickering lanterns and the glow of the sunset, a personal butler will serve you a bespoke 5-course tasting menu paired with champagne, creating memories that will last a lifetime.

The Azure Voyage

Timing: 4:30 PM - 8:30 PM (Pre-booking required) Cost: ₹15,000 per person

Board our private luxury yacht for an unparalleled dining expedition. As you sail gently along the serene Andaman coastline, watch the sky erupt in colors while enjoying gourmet canapés. Once anchored in a secluded cove, your private chef prepares a spectacular dinner under the starry night sky.
