Island Weddings at The Riviera

Exchange vows where the turquoise waters of the Andaman Sea become your backdrop. Our private island offers complete seclusion — from barefoot ceremonies on coral-white sand, to candlelit dinners under a constellation of stars. Every detail is curated by our dedicated wedding team to reflect your love story.

Signature Ceremony Venue

The Coral Beach

Our private Coral Beach is the island's most coveted setting for ceremonies. A crescent of powdery white sand lined with swaying palms, it offers sweeping views across the Andaman Sea and complete privacy for you and your loved ones.

Accommodates up to 200 guests for a ceremony and 120 for an open-air reception dinner. Ideal for traditional Indian mandap setups, sunrise ceremonies, and torch-lit receptions.

Intimate Garden Setting

The Orchid Pavilion

Nestled within our fragrant tropical gardens, the Orchid Pavilion is a semi-open thatched structure for couples who want shade and lush greenery without sacrificing the magic of the outdoors.

Soft lanterns, handwoven island décor, and the scent of frangipani make this setting deeply romantic.
